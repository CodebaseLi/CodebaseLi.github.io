Biography

I am currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree with the Web Information Processing Lab, Peking University, under the supervision of Prof. Dongyan Zhao.

My research interests broadly include Natural Language Generation, Summarization, and Dialogue. Now, I am also committed to the frontier exploration and practical application of Large Language Models (LLM) and Artificial Intelligence Generated Content (AIGC).

I have published several papers in top-tier conferences such as AAAI, ACL, WWW and NeurIPS.
