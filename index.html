I am currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree with the Web Information Processing Lab, Peking University, under the supervision of Prof. Dongyan Zhao.

My research interests broadly include Large Language Models (LLM), Artificial Intelligence Generated Content (AIGC) and Natural Language Generation (NLG).

I have published several papers in top-tier conferences such as AAAI, ACL, WWW and NeurIPS, and served as the program chair at NLP/AI conferences.
