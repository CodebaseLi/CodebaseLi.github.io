 Dept. of AI, Peking University, Beijing, China.

I obtained the Ph.D. degree from the Web Information Processing Lab, Peking University in 2024.07, under the supervision of Prof. Dongyan Zhao and Prof. Rui Yan. My research interests broadly include Large Language Models (LLM), Multimodal Large Model (MLM) and Artificial Intelligence Generated Content (AIGC). I have served as the program chair at NLP/AI conferences and published 10+ papers in top-tier conferences such as NeurIPS, ACL, WWW, EMNLP and AAAI.
